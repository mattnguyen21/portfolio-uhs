The Dark Kingdom is one of our newest parks in our Kingdom. It only opens on Halloween, when all the haunting and the undead comes out..

 

On this ride, people get to experience what it's like to have fear. Fear is the most common enemy in our society and it must be stopped. You have a fear of being in the dark with fast transportation? This is the ride for you.

Your physical and mental health will be challenged in our chainsaw massacre. Each and every one of you must face your darkest fears, otherwise you will be sawed in half.

Our oldest ride is one of the largest rollercoaster in the world. In this rollercoaster you will travel at high speed towards your worst nightmare. This ride goes at 150mph at launch, and ends with 91mph. Hold on to your skins and bones, cause you are going to need it.

Lastly, this ride is your free ticket to your new world. It may be just a dream to you, or it may be that you are really traveling to another dimension, with things waiting to haunt you.
